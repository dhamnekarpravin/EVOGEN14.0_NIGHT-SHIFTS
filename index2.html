"Power BI Insights: Visualize, Analyze, Dominate."

"Welcome to the 'Data to Dashboard' challenge, a competition designed to test and enhance Power BI skills. Participants will collect data from online sources, clean and prepare it, and then use Power BI to create interactive dashboards that reveal key insights and present to a non-technical person. This event focuses on practical application, allowing students to demonstrate their ability to use Power BI for data analysis, visualization, and effective communication of findings. Judges will evaluate the clarity, interactivity, and impact of the dashboards, as well as the students' presentation skills."

Power BI Insights Challenge: Competition Rules

Participants must select one topic among the following 3 topics

Participants are responsible for collecting their own data from reputable online sources

Data must be relevant to the chosen topic

Participants must use Power BI Desktop to create their dashboards and visualizations

No pre-built templates or dashboards are allowed

Participants will have to present their dashboards and findings to the judges

Participants must submit their Power BI (.pbix) file and presentation slides by night itself

(NOTE): Submitted file must be presented in the final round

Participants must have access to a computer capable of running Power BI desktop

The organizers reserve the right to amend these rules as necessary
